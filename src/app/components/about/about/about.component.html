The Union

Sagamu Union is a congregation of Sagamu sons and daughters living in Great Britain, formed to project good image of Sagamu and promote its interest and that of all her descendants both home and abroad, in all aspects of life at all levels.

Though the Union was first launched in 1961, it was revived on January 30th, 2000, with its first meeting sponsored by Alhaji Bashir Dauda (Agbegilere) and was re-launched on October 5th, 2002.
