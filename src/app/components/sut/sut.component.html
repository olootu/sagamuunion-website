What is SUT?

SUT is an acronym for Sagamu Union Tomorrow. It is a section within the Union, specifically created to promote the younger adults and also to act as the youthful face of the Sagamu Union (GB).

Who Can Join?

Every member of the union between the ages of 18 - 49 years old automatically belongs to this group, as well as being a member of the Union.

Current Initiatives of the SUT

  • Yoruba Lessons: In order to promote the spread of the Yoruba language, the SUT is organising the Yoruba lessons for our youths and children. Please email sut@sagamuunion.org for more details.
  • Free Business Hub: Are you a member and want to promote your trade or business, the SUT has created a FREE Business Hub where you can advertise your business/trade.
