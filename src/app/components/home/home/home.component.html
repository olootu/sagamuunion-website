Providing for the needy

Our main objectives are to cater for the welfare of our members, to embark on different projects that will uplift the standard of living of our people back home and also to promote our great town, SAGAMU.

Our projects

The primary project of the union is the Remo Enable Centre for the Blind located at Fakoya street, Sabo, Sagamu.

Caring for the Youth
